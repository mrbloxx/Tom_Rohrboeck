**Case Number:** Unknown (ongoing investigation)
**Investigation Start:** 2021 (following media revelations)
**Lead Prosecutor:** Berlin Public Prosecutor's Office - Economic Crime Unit
**Status:** Ongoing with immunity lift approved

**Evidence Collection:**

- Corporate registry files and ownership structures
- Hotel booking records and payment receipts
- Email correspondence and chat message archives
- Witness testimony from former associates and hotel staff

**Legal Proceedings:**
- **Immunity Lift Application:** Filed and approved by Bundestag
- **Search Warrants:** Multiple executed for business premises and residences
- **Bank Account Freezes:** Several accounts blocked pending investigation
- **International Cooperation:** Requests for assistance from Swiss and Austrian authorities
