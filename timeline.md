# Tom Rohrböck Investigation Timeline

## 2021 - Major Investigations Break

### June 2021 - Media Storm
**June 23, 2021**
- NDR/WDR broadcast "Das rechte Phantom" on Panorama die Reporter
- First major public revelation of Rohrböck's network
- Alice Weidel confirms contact with "about half" of AfD faction
- Documentation of 40+ Bundestag representative connections

**June 26, 2021**
- DIE ZEIT publishes comprehensive investigation "Der Schattenmann der AfD"
- 3-year research project revealed
- Evidence of luxury hotel stays with Alice Weidel
- Shell company network documentation
- Strategic political interference evidence

**Late June 2021**
- STRG_F releases YouTube documentary (932K+ views)
- Perspektive Online publishes economic analysis
- Multiple German media outlets pick up story
- AfD announces internal investigation

### Internal AfD Response (2021)
- Carsten Hütter (Bundesschatzmeister) calls for internal investigation
- AfD leadership acknowledges financial offers from Rohrböck
- Party promises "full clarification"
- Multiple state-level inquiries initiated

## 2019-2020 - Peak Influence Operations

### 2019 - Bavarian AfD Leadership
**Early 2019**
- Rohrböck encourages Corinna Miazga to run for Bavarian leadership
- Strategic planning begins

**Pre-Election (2019)**
- Chat messages to opponent: "Ich denke nicht, dass du kandidieren solltest"
- Follow-up message: "Du läufst in eine Falle. Mehr als 30 Prozent bekommst du nicht"
- Opponent withdraws candidacy 3 days before election
- Miazga wins leadership position

### 2020 - Baden-Württemberg Mediation
**2020**
- Conflict emerges between Alice Weidel and Dirk Spaniel
- Rohrböck intervenes as mediator
- "De-escalating" influence on leadership conflict
- Compromise solution brokered
- Spaniel confirms Rohrböck's mediation role

### 2018 - Shell Company Operations

## 2022-2025 - Russian Intelligence Integration

### 2022 - Systematic Intelligence Gathering
**Post-Ukraine Invasion**
- AfD MPs begin systematic parliamentary inquiries
- Focus on military and security information
- Pattern emerges of coordinated questioning

### 2025 - Intelligence Operations Exposed
**June 2025**
- Ringo Mühlmann files 8 drone-related inquiries in Thuringia
- Questions about police drone defense systems
- Technical specifications requested
- Operational capabilities sought

**September 2025**
- Mühlmann requests military transport data
- Detailed breakdown of arms shipments through Thuringia
- Route information and security procedures
- Timeline: 2022-2025 transit data

**December 2025**
- POLITICO publishes comprehensive investigation
- Georg Maier (SPD) accuses AfD of working from "Kremlin task list"
- International attention on Russian intelligence connections
- Hybrid warfare implications documented
